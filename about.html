About Us

At HappyTails, we believe that every pet deserves a loving and caring home. Our mission is to connect abandoned, rescued, or surrendered pets with compassionate families who can give them a second chance at happiness. Guided by our passion for animal welfare, we work tirelessly to create a future where every furry friend has a place to call home.

Our Mission and Vision

A Safe Haven for Every Paw

We are committed to rescuing pets in need and ensuring their health and wellbeing. Through a network of devoted volunteers and foster families, we provide temporary homes, medical care, and emotional support to animals before they find their forever families. Our vision is a world where shelters are empty because every pet has found a loving family.
